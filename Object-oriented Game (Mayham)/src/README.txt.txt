Hi, and welcome to the clash of trutles.
In this battle between two races of turtle, 
you controle the turtles that are equipped with a fuel powered jetpack.  

To controls:

- Player 1
	LEFT    = A
	RIGHT   = D
	FORWARD = W
	SHOOT   = V

- Player 2
	LEFT    = L_ARROW
	RIGHT   = R_ARROW
	FORWARD = F_ARROW
	SHOOT   = M
